The Ultimate Swan Valley Brewery Trail

Just 25 minutes north-east of the Perth CBD, the Swan Valley is famous for its vineyards — but it's also home to one of Australia's densest corridors of independent craft breweries. This curated loop groups stops that sit only minutes apart, so you spend less time driving and more time drinking.

Full Day Trip
~30km Loop
Beer Lovers, Families & Weekend Road-Trippers

    Homestead Brewery

    Caversham · Mandoon Estate

    Best for: breakfast & a refined vibe

    Henley Brook

    Best for: award-winning IPAs & a pub lunch

    Henley Brook

  4. 4

    Baskerville · home of Slumdog Brewing

    Best for: the final icon — Feral's Biggie Juice & Hop Hog, still on tap
