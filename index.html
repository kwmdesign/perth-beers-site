The Brewery Trail

Three distinct patches, three distinct pours. Pick one and start exploring.

Seasonal Releases

WA-grown hops and native botanicals — quandong, lemon myrtle, Geraldton wax — turn up in small-batch drops timed to the state's seasons. Crisp dry-summer lagers give way to richer ales once the Hills cool down.

Community & Events

Trivia nights, brewery tours, beer-and-food-truck pairings, and the odd backyard festival. WA's taprooms are built for lingering — most have a playground or a beer garden, and almost all are dog-friendly.

  • Weekly taproom trivia & live music
  • Rotating food truck line-ups
  • Seasonal beer festivals across the valley
